Brand information:

Green Saffron is a distinguished purveyor of the finest spices, rice, sauces, and spice blends, founded by Anglo-Indian food entrepreneur and spice expert Arun Kapil. The company is known for its commitment to quality, authenticity, and sustainability, sourcing its spices directly from partner farms in India that adhere to ethical and sustainable farming practices. These spices are EU compliant, gluten-free, and available both as single-source and new-season products, ensuring the highest grade of freshness and safety. Green Saffron's unique supply chain provides full transparency and traceability, allowing customers a direct line-of-sight to the Indian farmers who cultivate their spices. Their product range is extensive, including both ground and whole spices, and is available for wholesale in various quantities—from kilos to containers—worldwide【5†source】【6†source】.

Arun Kapil, the founder, is not only the driving force behind Green Saffron's success but also an advocate for the power of spices to transform cooking. Kapil's passion for the science of spice and flavor combinations shines through in the company's innovative products. He is a well-respected figure in the culinary world, sharing his spice expertise through cookbooks, television appearances, and food festivals, encouraging home cooks and professional chefs alike to explore the rich flavors of Indian spices【7†source】.

The company is dedicated to corporate social responsibility, supporting sustainable and ethical practices across its operations【8†source】.
